Music

We all love music. Whether it has lyrics or its an instrumental, there will always been songs or musical pieces that will speak to us; with the capacity to express what we sometimes feel we cannot. This is one of the reasons we often feel soothed after listening.

Here are three genres worth checking out:

Slow Instrumental Jazz

No words, just vibes. Listening slow intrumental jazz has been found to improve sleep, memory and mood, encourage creativity, reduce depression, as well as aid in post-operation recovery and pain reduction. Check out this soothing number from the Relax Jazz Piano channel on YouTube.

Native American Flute Music

Often listened to during meditation, flute music is known to calm body and mind, lowering heat rate and reduce physical pain. Give a listen to this beautiful piece from multi-Grammy nominated artist, Carlos Nakai.

Solfeggio Based (New Age) Music

The nine Solfeggio frequencies, are believed to aid in anything from calming the mind to removing negative energies trapped in body to eliminating feelings of guilt. Solfeggion frequency 528 Hz, for example is believed to bring miracles and transformation.
